Khalid

In "Shot Down", Khalid recounts the tale of an all-consuming love that he surrendered to completely. With an unhurried snare pattern and finger snaps setting the pace, the production highlights Khalid’s soulful, sincere vocals, which despite their power stop short of being overwrought. While it’s easy to hear “Shot Down” as a conventional song to a lover, it could just as easily be about Khalid’s devotion to his art: the beautiful, heartfelt ballads that are sure to keep knocking you off your feet.
